Casablanca

Casablanca is the largest city in Morocco and is known for its modern architecture and vibrant culture.

Rabat

Rabat is the capital city of Morocco, famous for its historical sites and beautiful coastal views.

Marrakech

Marrakech is renowned for its bustling markets, stunning palaces, and vibrant nightlife.

Fès

Fès is known for its ancient medina, traditional crafts, and rich cultural heritage.
